1 Year Abadhik Bachat


1 year abadhik bachat is the fixed deposit product. As in the name it is for 1 year. Individual can take Abadhik Bachat Rasid Dhito when they need money before the maturity date. In 1 year interest rate is 8% and if the individual has mahila bachat or is more than 60 yrs old then interest rate is increased by 0.50%. All the individuals or group can do 1 year abadhik bachat, being member is not necessary.
